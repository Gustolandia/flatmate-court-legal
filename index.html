Privacy Policy

How the Flatmate Court app handles your information.

Effective date: 13 June 2026  ·  Last updated: 14 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Ricou Developments (“we”, “us”) handles information in connection with the Flatmate Court mobile application (the “App”). Flatmate Court is a light-hearted entertainment app for settling household disputes. It is not a real legal service and provides no legal advice.

The short version. Flatmate Court works entirely on your device. We do not run accounts, and your cases, the names you enter, your house laws and your settings never leave your phone — we cannot see them. The only data that ever leaves the device is (1) anonymous crash diagnostics and (2) data collected by our advertising provider, both described below. The App continues to work fully offline; with no internet connection, no ads are shown and nothing is sent anywhere.

1. Information stored only on your device

Everything you create in the App — the cases you file, the (real or fake) names of parties, verdicts, your saved casebook, custom house laws, and your humour/theme settings — is stored locally on your device only. It is never uploaded to us or to any server. We have no access to it. You can erase it at any time from Settings → Clear Casebook, or by clearing the App’s data / uninstalling the App.

2. Information that leaves the device

a) Crash diagnostics (Sentry)

To find and fix bugs, the App may send anonymous crash and error diagnostics to Sentry (Functional Software, Inc.). These reports contain technical information such as the type of error, a stack trace, the App version, your device model and operating-system version. They do not include your cases, the names you entered, or any content you created. Crash data for this App is processed in the European Union (Sentry’s EU region). See Sentry’s privacy policy.

b) Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App may show occasional advertisements supplied by Google AdMob (Google Ireland Limited / Google LLC). To serve ads, Google may collect and process information including a device advertising identifier, IP address, and general device and usage information. This is governed by Google’s policies, not ours:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services

Ads are shown only when the device is online. If the device is offline, no ads are requested and no advertising data is collected; the App still works normally.

3. Consent (EEA, UK & Switzerland)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom or Switzerland, the App presents a consent message (via Google’s User Messaging Platform) before personalised ads are shown, in line with applicable data-protection and ePrivacy rules. You may choose non-personalised ads where offered, and you can change your choice later from your device’s Google ad settings.

4. Legal bases for processing (GDPR)

  • Consent — for personalised advertising and any associated identifiers.
  • Legitimate interests — for anonymous crash diagnostics used solely to keep the App stable and secure.

5. Children

Flatmate Court is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Data retention

Content you create stays on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App. Crash diagnostics are retained by Sentry for a limited period under their standard retention settings. Advertising data is retained by Google under its own policies.

7. Your rights

Because the content you create is stored only on your device, you are in direct control of it and can delete it at any time (see Section 1). Depending on your location (e.g. under the GDPR), you may also have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of personal data, and to object to processing or withdraw consent. For data held by our providers, you can also exercise rights directly with Google and Sentry via the links above. To make a request to us, use the contact details below.
